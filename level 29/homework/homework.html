About the series

  • What is JoJo's bizarre adventure?

    • JoJo's Bizarre Adventure (JJBA) is a long-running Japanese manga and anime series created by Hirohiko Araki. The story follows the adventures of the Joestar family across multiple generations, each having a protagonist whose name is a variation of "JoJo." With its blend of supernatural powers, detailed artwork, and over-the-top battles, the series is renowned for its creativity and influence on pop culture.

Main story arcs

  • Phantom Blood (Part 1)

    • The origin of the Joestar family saga begins in the 19th century. Jonathan Joestar, a nobleman, faces off against his adoptive brother, Dio Brando, whose thirst for power leads to a deadly transformation.

  • Battle Tendency (Part 2)

    • Joseph Joestar, the grandson of Jonathan, faces an ancient threat from powerful beings called the Pillar Men. Using his wits and his extraordinary fighting spirit, Joseph must save the world from their sinister plans.

  • Stardust Crusaders (Part 3)

    • In the 1980s, Jotaro Kujo discovers his Stand, Star Platinum, and joins a group of allies to stop Dio Brando, who has returned with his own Stand, The World, to conquer the world.

  • Diamond is Unbreakable (Part 4)

    • In 1990s Morioh, Josuke Higashikata, the son of Joseph Joestar, protects his town from Stand users, including the deadly Yoshikage Kira, whose Stand, Killer Queen, turns anything into a bomb.

  • Golden Wind (Part 5)

    • In 1990s Italy, Giorno Giovanna, the son of Dio, seeks to take control of the mafia group Passione to reform it. He battles mafia boss Diavolo with his Stand, Gold Experience.

  • Stone Ocean (Part 6)

    • In the 2010s, Jolyne Cujoh, the daughter of Jotaro, is wrongfully imprisoned. She must stop Enrico Pucci, who seeks to rewrite reality and create a perfect world using his Stand, Whitesnake.

  • Steel Ball Run (Part 7)

    • In the 1890s, Johnny Joestar enters the Steel Ball Run, a cross-country race across the U.S., seeking the Saint's Corpse and the power it holds, while learning the mysterious Spin technique.

  • JoJolion (Part 8)

    • In an alternate universe, Josuke Higashikata (a different Josuke) suffers from amnesia and uncovers the strange happenings in Morioh, as he tries to piece together his identity amidst dangerous Stand user
